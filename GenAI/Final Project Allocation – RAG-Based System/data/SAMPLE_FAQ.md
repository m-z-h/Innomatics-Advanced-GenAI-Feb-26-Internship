# Customer Support FAQ

## Account Management

### How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, follow these steps:
1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password?"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for a reset link
5. Click the link and enter your new password
6. Your password has been reset

If you don't receive the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.

### How do I enable two-factor authentication (2FA)?
Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security to your account. To enable 2FA:
1. Log into your account
2. Go to Settings > Security
3. Click "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
4. Choose your method:
   - SMS (text message codes)
   - Authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Microsoft Authenticator)
5. Follow the setup instructions
6. Save your backup codes in a safe place

Once enabled, you'll need to provide a code each time you log in.

### How do I change my email address?
To update your email address:
1. Go to Settings > Account Information
2. Click "Edit Email"
3. Enter your new email address
4. We'll send a confirmation link to the new address
5. Click the link to confirm the change

You must confirm your new email within 24 hours, or the change will be cancelled.

### How do I delete my account?
To permanently delete your account:
1. Go to Settings > Account
2. Scroll to "Delete Account"
3. Click "Delete Account"
4. Confirm that you understand this is permanent
5. Your account will be deleted immediately

Note: This cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed.

## Technical Support

### What should I do if the app is running slowly?
If you're experiencing slow performance:
1. Clear your browser cache and cookies
2. Try a different browser
3. Check your internet connection
4. Disable browser extensions that might interfere
5. Restart your browser

If the problem persists, contact support.

### How do I report a bug?
To report a bug:
1. Go to Settings > Help > Report a Bug
2. Describe the issue as clearly as possible
3. Include steps to reproduce the bug
4. Attach a screenshot if possible
5. Submit your report

Our technical team will investigate and respond within 24 hours.

### What if I'm getting an error message?
Try these troubleshooting steps:
1. Clear your browser cache
2. Log out and log back in
3. Try a different browser
4. Try on a different device
5. Check if there are any system status updates

If the error persists, note the exact error message and contact support.

- AES-256 encryption for data at rest
- Regular security audits
- Compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and SOC 2
- Two-factor authentication support

Your privacy is our priority.
